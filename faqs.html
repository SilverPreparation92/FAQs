FAQs

1. What devices does this remote app support?
Our app is compatible with most smart TVs, including Samsung, LG, Roku, Android TV, and Fire TV. Compatibility may vary depending on your TV model and software version.
2. Does my phone need to be on the same Wi-Fi network as the TV?
Yes, for most features to work, both your phone and TV must be connected to the same Wi-Fi network.
3. Can I use this app without an internet connection?
An internet connection is required initially to pair your device. Some functions may be limited without an active connection.
4. Why isn't my TV showing up in the app?
Make sure your TV is turned on, connected to the same Wi-Fi as your phone, and that network discovery is enabled on the TV. Restarting both devices can also help.
5. How do I pair my TV with the app?
Once both devices are on the same network, open the app and it will automatically scan for nearby TVs. Tap on your TV to connect.
6. Is the app free to use?
Yes, the app is free with basic features. Premium features can be unlocked with a one-time purchase or subscription.
7. What should I do if the app stops working?
Try restarting your phone and TV, ensure both are on the same Wi-Fi, and update the app to the latest version.
